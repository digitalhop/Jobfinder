    My client, a global telecoms business based in Bracknell have partnered with Michael Page in their search for an RPA Analyst to revolutionise their business processes as they implement both UI Path and Power BI.

    Job Description

    This role sits within the newly created Centre of Excellence which supports Sales, Marketing, Operations and Finance - with a particular focus on Demand and Supply Planning. Reporting into the Head of Inventory you will be tasked with working with senior stakeholders to explore different ways of solving business problems using automation. The business have recently invested in UI Path as their RPA tool of choice and along with the Head of Inventory you will be the SME for this tool in the business. In terms of what they need from you they are looking for someone with 1 years' experience using an RPA Tool (UI Path is the preferred) along with good T-SQL and a problem-solving mindset. It's a great time to join the business as they're currently investing heavily in technology and are in the process of implementing Power BI and this is another tool you will be able to pick up should you wish.
